Biography

Since I was young, I have always been interested in the world of computing. I started off slow, forming simple word documents about how much I like cats and until now, where I have been able to build computers from scratch and even developing my own websites. For the last 5 years I have been a customer service representative for Coles Supermarkets, serving the local community their essentials, even during the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Within those 5 years I have also been in and out of university, coming to find a great interest in web development, admiring the innovation of major brands like Samsung, ServiceNow and Google. Aside from education and work, at heart I am a big music and movie buff. Loving the melodies and sounds of artists like Lorde, Billie Eilish and Dua Lipa, to the theatrical masterpieces of Taika Waititi and Christopher Nolan.

Visions, Goals & Missions

I hope to work for a positive, sustainable company be it big or small. I would like to be part of or contribute to innovation and evidently be at the forefront of cutting-edge technology. My long-term goal is to hopefully become a senior UX/UI designer, creating my own projects that could help and inspire current and future generations.
