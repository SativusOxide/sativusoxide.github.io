Teletrack Maintaince Module

Overview

As part of an extra offering for the already existing Teletrack platform, I was tasked with bringing to life the Maintenance Module. The aim of this project was to allow existing and future clients of Teletrack to access a wide variety of features that could digitise, automate and simplify the maintenance process for fleet assets like forklifts, diggers and cars.
